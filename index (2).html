  UPCITE EVENTS

HACKATHON

        Hackathon is where the IT Students had a programming contest that was held around the Pangasinan province. The programmers showcased their talents and the reason why the hackathon was held was because they wanted an asset that will give ease to the PNP.

CITE EXHIBIT

        The CITE Exhibit showed how capable and committed the university's IT students had remained as they paved their way onto the technological field, creating apps and perfecting their talents beyond their capabilities. A true exCITEment fulfilled for the CITE Application Exhibit this year!

CITE FEST

        The CITE Fest is an annual event held by the College of Information Technology Education Department. Activities under it are the Sports Fest wherein it consists of various physical sports for the CITE students to participate in Basketball, Volleyball, Badminton, and Chess. These activities made our students cooperate with each other and communicate with each member. Another activity is the ESports wherein the students showcase their gaming skills battling out to see whose the best team in various games such as valorant, mobile legends, etc. Lastly is the Komsayahan the major highlight of the whole CITE Fest wherein it showcase students with their creative cosplays and outstanding performances.
